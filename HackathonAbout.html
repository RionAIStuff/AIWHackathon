Why an Internal AI Hackathon?

The RTS AI Hackathon is more than just an event; it's a catalyst for innovation and collaboration. By bringing together diverse perspectives during a weekend summit, we aim to foster teamwork and enhance the AI skills of our employees. This internal hackathon is specifically designed to accommodate busy schedules, ensuring broader participation and a dynamic platform for knowledge exchange.

We believe in breaking down silos and encouraging cross-functional collaboration. This event will not only strengthen team cohesion but also propel our company's AI services forward, fostering a culture of continuous learning and technological advancement. The hackathon serves as a nexus for exchanging ideas, identifying emerging trends, and positioning RTS at the forefront of AI technology advancements.

Join us in this exciting journey to drive RTS towards excellence in AI services, empowering our team to innovate and grow in the rapidly evolving AI landscape.
